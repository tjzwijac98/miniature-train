19th Century Model Trains

Did you know that the first model trains were first manufactured in 1891 by, a Germen manufacture, Marklin?

Before Marklin built model trains, there were things called Carpet Railways. The first documented carpet railways was built by French Emperor Napoleon III for his son in the 1840s.

Early 20th Century Model Trains

In 1901 the first electric model trains were made by Lionel. You can read more about the history of Lionel Trains here.

After the introduction of electric model trains, the demand for model trains became higher resulting in the golden age of trains from the 1920s until the 1940s.
